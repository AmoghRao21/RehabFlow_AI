# 🎬 RehabFlow AI — 3-Minute Demo Video Script

## Setup
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080
- **Browser:** Chrome, localhost:7860 open
- **Mode:** Demo Mode (no model needed)
- **Pre-saved:** One sample plan already saved for the "My Plans" tab

---

## 0:20–1:10 — Create a Plan (50s)
**[Screen: Tab 1 — Create New Plan]**

1. Click **"🦵 Knee Osteoarthritis"** example button (auto-fills form)
2. Show the auto-filled fields: patient name, condition, symptoms, pain level slider
3. Optionally upload the `knee_pain.jpg` sample image
4. Click **"🧠 Generate Rehab Plan"**
5. Scroll through the generated plan — highlight:
   - Patient Overview section
   - 14-Day exercise table with sets/reps/form cues
   - Safety & Red Flags section
   - Disclaimer
6. Click **"💾 Save Plan"**

> "With one click, we get a structured, safety-first 14-day plan with progressive exercises, form cues, and red-flag screening — all tailored to this patient."

---

## 1:10–1:50 — Progress Tracking (40s)
**[Screen: Tab 2 — Log Daily Progress]**

1. Click **"🔄 Refresh Plans List"**
2. Select the saved knee plan from dropdown
3. Set pain slider to **3/10** (improvement from original 6)
4. Type: "Feeling much better, stairs are easier. Slight morning stiffness still."
5. Click **"🔄 Update My Plan"**
6. Show the adapted plan with:
   - Progress assessment
   - Adjusted exercises (progressed since pain decreased)
   - Updated recommendations

> "The AI recognizes improved pain levels and automatically progresses the exercise program — just like a real follow-up visit."

---

## 1:50–2:30 — My Plans + PDF Export (40s)
**[Screen: Tab 3 — My Plans]**

1. Click **"🔄 Refresh"** to show saved plans
2. Select a plan from dropdown
3. Show full plan details with update history
4. Click **"📄 Download PDF"**
5. Open the downloaded PDF — show professional formatting:
   - Blue header with branding
   - Patient info box
   - Disclaimer
   - Clean body text

> "Patients can download their complete plan as a professional PDF — ready to print, share with their therapist, or reference offline."
